üßø About TRR
The Roshan Rumble (TRR) is a community-run Dota 2 tournament series focused on structure, fairness and fun. Every season is built around:
  • Captains selected by admins and a live auction to build balanced teams.
  • Dedicated Discord with team voice channels, activity checks and clear rules.
  • Anti-smurf investigation system and strict stand-in rules to keep games fair.
TRR is for players who want a proper league experience instead of random, chaotic lobbies.
